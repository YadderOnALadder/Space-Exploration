FUSE: NASA Mission

FUSE (Far Ultraviolet Spectroscopic Explorer) was made in order to study space in a high-quality satelite/telescope.

FAST: NASA Mission

Fast Auroral Snapshot Explorer (FAST) was made to investigate behavior of plasma and particles in auroras. Auroras are formed when solar material causes a disturbance in Earth space in a cloud form called "coronal mass enjection" or variations in the sun's constant outflow of solar wind.
