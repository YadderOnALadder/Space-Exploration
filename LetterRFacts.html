Nancy Grace Roman Space Telescope

The Nancy Grace Roman Space Telescope has 3 main purposes. Its first purpose will be about studying dark energy. The second purpose was about measuring the history of cosmic acceleration. The third purpose was searching for worlds way beyond the solar system and demonstrate state of the art tech which is for direct imaging and characterizing distant exoplanets.

Ranger Series: Spacecrafts

When the early 1690's hit, NASA manufactured a series of "Ranger" spacecrafts in order about studying The Moon. They were also the first American spacecrafts to land on The Moon which in turn set the foundation for the Apollo program.
