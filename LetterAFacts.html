Astronaut "Chris Austin Hadfield"

Astronaut "Chris Austin Hadfield" is a retired engineer, musician, and fighter pilot. He was an astronaut from 1978 to 2003, he was also ranked a "Colonel" in the Canadian Forces.

Chris Austin Hadfield was born on August 29, 1959. He grew up in the town Sarnia, Ontario with his two parents Roger and Eleanor Hadfield. Chris Hadfield took an interest for airplanes and flying in general at a young age so he decided to be a pilot.

Artemis Mission

The "Artemis Mission" is that astronauts are going back to the moon to get resources from it, such as fuel, materials, and other resources. This mission is also for exploring the moon's surface for scientific purposes and research.
