OSIRIS-REx: NASA Mission

The OSIRIS-REx (Origins Spectral Interpretation Resource Identification Security - Regolith Explorer) is a NASA Mission that was launched in order to grab samples from an asteroid near Earth named Bennu. The OSIRIS-REx reached Bennu in 2018, and is expected to deliver samples to Earth this year.

Orion Spacecraft

Orion Spacecraft is a NASA Mission that was created to deliver humans in space farther than ever delivered before. As of now, there is no launch date for Orion.
