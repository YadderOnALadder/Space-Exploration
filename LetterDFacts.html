DART (Double Asteroid Redirection Test)

DART (Double Asteroid Redirection Test) is a mission where a small spacecraft was on course to try to redirect a asteroid. DART ended up being very helpful to NASA's asteroid redirection research.

Dawn Mission

The "Dawn" mission was a mission that started in 2007 and ended in 2018. The "Dawn" mission was a mission where a spacecraft was on course to Ceres and Vesta to explore these dwarf planets.
