PACE: NASA Mission

PACE (Plankton, Aerosol, Cloud, ocean Ecosystem) is a NASA Mission created in order to understand how the oceans and atmospheres exchange CO2 (Carbon Dioxide), as well as revealing how maybe aerosols will fuel phytoplankton growth on the ocean's surface.

Phoenix Mars Lander

The Phoenix Mars Lander was built in order to explore the unknown history of the martian waters. The NASA Mission was launched on Aug. 4, 2007 and the mission reached Mars on May 25, 2008.
